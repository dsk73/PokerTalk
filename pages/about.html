About PokerTalk

Your trusted companion in the world of poker — from strategies to tournaments and news.

Our Mission

PokerTalk was built to connect poker enthusiasts across India and beyond. Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, our goal is to deliver top-tier strategies, analysis, tournament coverage, and a thriving poker community.

How We Started

Founded in 2024 by a group of passionate players, PokerTalk began as a forum. It quickly grew into a hub for discussions, news, and learning. Today, it reaches thousands of poker lovers worldwide.

What We Offer

  • Daily poker news & tournament recaps
  • Expert-led strategy articles & videos
  • Live & online event coverage
  • Interactive player forums
  • Merchandise and gear
